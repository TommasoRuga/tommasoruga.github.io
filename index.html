Tommaso Ruga

Phd Student @ DIMES Department, University of Calabria

Tommaso Ruga is a Ph.D. Student in Information and Communication Technologies at the Department of Computer Engineering, Modeling, Electronics, and Systems Engineering (DIMES) of the University of Calabria, Italy. He graduated in Computer Engineering in 2023 with a thesis on skin lesion images classification. The main topic of its current research is to provide AI solutions for public administration, which also includes various public domains such as healthcare, agricolture and cultural heritage. Its research is focused not only on providing effective solutions, but also on ensuring that these solutions are explainable - both from the perspective of domain experts and technical specialists. Furthermore, it is committed to studying the fairness and ethical implications of the proposed solutions, with particular attention to the biases present in the datasets currently used to train models.
